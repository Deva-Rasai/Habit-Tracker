Exercise Library

Surya Namaskar (Sun Salutation)

Benefits: Full-body warm-up, improves flexibility, energizes the body

Sets: 5-12 rounds

Instructions: Flow through 12 postures smoothly, coordinating breath with movement. Start slow and build speed as you warm up.

🧘 Yoga ⏱️ 10-15 mins

Dumbbell Shoulder Press

Benefits: Builds shoulder strength, improves upper body stability

Sets: 3 sets × 10-12 reps

Instructions: Stand with feet shoulder-width apart. Press dumbbells overhead until arms are fully extended. Lower with control.

🏋️ Strength ⏱️ 8-10 mins

Resistance Band Chest Fly

Benefits: Targets chest muscles, improves shoulder mobility

Sets: 3 sets × 12-15 reps

Instructions: Anchor band behind you. Hold handles and bring arms together in front of chest. Focus on squeezing chest muscles.

💪 Resistance ⏱️ 6-8 mins

Dumbbell Bicep Curls

Benefits: Isolates bicep muscles, improves arm strength

Sets: 3 sets × 10-12 reps

Instructions: Stand with dumbbells at sides. Curl weights up while keeping elbows stationary. Lower with control.

🏋️ Strength ⏱️ 6-8 mins

Band Pull-Apart

Benefits: Strengthens upper back, improves posture, shoulder health

Sets: 3 sets × 15-20 reps

Instructions: Hold band at shoulder height. Pull apart by squeezing shoulder blades together. Return to start slowly.

💪 Resistance ⏱️ 5-7 mins

Dumbbell Squats

Benefits: Builds leg strength, improves core stability

Sets: 3 sets × 12-15 reps

Instructions: Hold dumbbells at sides. Lower into squat keeping chest up and knees tracking over toes. Drive through heels to stand.

🏋️ Strength ⏱️ 8-10 mins

Dumbbell Rows

Benefits: Strengthens back muscles, improves pulling strength

Sets: 3 sets × 10-12 reps each side

Instructions: Hinge at hips, support with one hand. Row dumbbell to hip, squeezing shoulder blade. Lower with control.

🏋️ Strength ⏱️ 8-10 mins

Full Body Stretching Routine

Benefits: Increases flexibility, reduces muscle tension, improves recovery

Duration: 15-20 minutes

Instructions: Hold each stretch for 20-30 seconds. Focus on major muscle groups: hamstrings, quads, shoulders, chest, and back.

🧘 Flexibility ⏱️ 15-20 mins
